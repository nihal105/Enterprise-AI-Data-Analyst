Business users often struggle to analyze large datasets because they require technical knowledge of SQL, Excel formulas, or Python.

This project develops an Enterprise AI Data Analyst that allows users to upload business datasets and ask analytical questions in natural language.

A custom Transformer model is trained from scratch to understand user intent and perform business analytics automatically. The system generates charts, KPIs, and business insights through an interactive Streamlit application.

The architecture is designed to be extensible with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) for enterprise document intelligence.
